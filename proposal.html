Project Proposal: Forest Guardians

Forest Guardians is a community-based environmental initiative focused on reforesting degraded areas in Tagbina, Surigao del Sur. The project addresses deforestation, biodiversity loss, and climate vulnerability through native tree planting, digital forest monitoring, and community education.

Partnering with SDSSU – Tagbina and local government units, the project empowers youth and residents to become forest stewards through ForestGuardians Youth Clubs, forest watch groups, and digital tools for tracking tree growth and reporting forest conditions.

With a clear methodology, defined scope, structured timeline, and sustainability plan, this proposal lays the groundwork for long-term forest restoration and community engagement.
